Welcome to this website dedicated to Life Direction. It's a joy to conduct these seminars all over the world. Under the different tabs you will find all you need to know about what it is and what is covered throughout the seminar. I recently returned from a weekend in the Middle East where I took a group of long term staff through the material. Listening to the stories of some who have been in Christian ministry for twenty to thirty years made me understand afresh that we're never done searching, exploring and understanding God's heart and plan.
A good friend in Latin America who hosted a weekend with sixty participants wrote: To be honest with you, although we knew of the potential of the Seminar, we had underestimated the results. Dozens of testimonies witnessed about the value of the seminar for people's lives. Many of them had never heard or understood exactly how they could fit in God's plan for His church. We believe, these "Basic" but new discoveries (in people's lives) will result in some being mobilized to be active in their local churches; some being challenged to do local evangelism; and some going beyond their countries.
The concern and passion for the growth and development of people drives and motivates me towards excellence in service. I am married to a beautiful and wonderful woman, called Martha. She is a great example and inspiration to me in the areas of servanthood, dedication and integrity; charceteristics I aspire to develop more in my own life, We have four children and seven grandchildren.
